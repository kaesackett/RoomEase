Welcome to RoomEase!

RoomEase is a web-app designed to make living with roommates easier by keeping track of your house's bills.



Login or Register now to be able to:

Keep track of your house's bills, and see which bills you have and have not paid your share of.



Send messages to your roommates via the house message board. Text reminders will be sent automatically to roommates that have unpaid bills with approaching due dates!

See all of the open bills for your house in Calendar View.
